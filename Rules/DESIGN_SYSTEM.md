# DESIGN SYSTEM

## Inspiration
Strictly Vercel, GitHub, Linear, Raycast.

## Colors
- Background: #000000
- Surface: #000000
- Border: #333333
- Primary: #EDEDED (White)
- Success: #238636 Warning: #D29922 Danger: #DA3633

## Typography
Primary: Geist / Inter
Mono: Geist Mono / IBM Plex Mono

## Radius
Tight/sharp. Cards:6px Inputs:6px Buttons:6px

## Spacing
4/8/12/16/24/32/48

## Shadows
None. Completely flat design.

## Motion
Hover:150ms Page:200ms Ease-out. Use micro-animations (e.g. group-hover scale).

## Principles
- Minimal & Flat
- Pitch Black Dark Mode only
- High Contrast
- Dense information
- Keyboard-first
- No shadows, glassmorphism, or neon gradients
